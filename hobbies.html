In the earlier days of my life I was very much passionate about sports

I was a football and cricket player

When I competed my B.tech, I was a very skinny guy. An I decided to work on my physical fitness

I was very Skinny guy from the very beginning. I was very foody but at the same time, I was losing a lot of calories due to more into sports. So the overall calorie intake was very less for my overall body. I was bullied a lot of times for my health and skinny physique. So after my b.tech, I decided not to take these any more and to do a transformation so that people can admire it. At that time, my age was 53kgs. So I joined a gym in my hometown and as suggested by the coach, started taking more calories in form of food. But as days passed on, I realized by watching youtube that these coaches might get you into wrong path. So I resumed my journey by watching online videos and reading fitness related articles. Then gradually I realized that more than bodybuilding, Fitness is more important. So for that I started counting my calories and focused on my diet as well. Now I am 74Kgs after 2 years. And I guess this is the result of my hard work and dedication.

So I am still pursuing the same bodybuilding passion and it has now become my hobby.

In my free time I used to read fitness related articles
